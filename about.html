About Me

I love a challenge. I love to take a simple pencil, piece of paper, and eraser, and make something really beautiful.

I am a traditionally trained artist, an oil painter, and a portraitist.

I love the individual challenges that every portrait brings: a head full of curls, a checked shirt, or a tartan dress.

I disappear into my work for hours at a time, completely entranced.

And I get excited about the finished result every single time.
